About CSK

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 2008, CSK has become one of the most loved and successful teams in the Indian Premier League.

  • Founded: 2008
  • Home Ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium
  • Captain: Ruturaj Gaikwad
  • Coach: Stephen Fleming
  • Nickname: Yellow Army
